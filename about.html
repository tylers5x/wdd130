History

Founded in 1995, our company began with a passion for adventure and a commitment to safety. Over the years, we have grown from a small local business to a well-known name in the rafting community. Our team of experienced guides and our dedication to providing an unforgettable experience have been the pillars of our success.

We started with just a few rafts and a dream, and now, we offer a variety of trips that cater to both beginners and seasoned rafters. From tranquil family-friendly floats to adrenaline-pumping white water adventures, we have something for everyone. Our history is rich with stories of excitement, challenge, and the joy of bringing people closer to nature.
